Ontario ESA formula: regular wages earned in the four work weeks before the work week with the holiday, plus the vacation pay payable on those wages, divided by 20. These are estimates — overtime pay is excluded per the ESA, and eligibility (e.g. the "last and first" rule) is not checked; you decide who qualifies. Ontario has 9 public holidays: New Year's Day, Family Day, Good Friday, Victoria Day, Canada Day, Labour Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas Day and Boxing Day.
