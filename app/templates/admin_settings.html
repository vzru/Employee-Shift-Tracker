Disclaimer: these are configurable estimates to help with payroll, not legal or payroll advice. Verify all rules against the current Ontario Employment Standards Act.

Unpaid break deduction

An unpaid break is only lawful if the employee actually took it and was free of duties. Use the per-shift override on the Shifts page for exceptions.

Overtime (Ontario ESA)

1.5× the regular rate for hours over the weekly threshold, calculated on a WORK WEEK basis (not daily). Note: genuine managers/supervisors may be exempt from overtime.

Minimum wage reference

Used to warn when an employee's pay rate is below it. Ontario general minimum wage is $17.60 as of Oct 1, 2025, rising to $17.95 on Oct 1, 2026.
